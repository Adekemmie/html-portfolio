The Best Movies According to Kemi Idowu

My top 3 Horror movies

Wednesday

My favorite movie. I love the dark wit, fantastic characters, and unmistakable Burtonesque aesthetic.

Scream

Awesome movie. Note: Don't watch if you hate horror.

The Pale Blue Eye

I love this one, it's a fictional story. The story has soft spots, but it mostly makes up for them with creepy atmosphere and some really impressive performances.
